When to run full audit

Use a full audit after major website updates, before demos, or when you want Lighthouse, accessibility, and content signals refreshed together.

When to run targeted scan

Use Accessibility when fixing WCAG issues, Content when reviewing copy and metadata quality, and Lighthouse only when you mainly care about score changes.

What happens next
  1. Start the scan here.
  2. Wait for status to move to completed.
  3. Open the website dashboard and issue pages to review updated findings.
